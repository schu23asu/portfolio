Poster Design (Adobe Illustrator)

A promotional event poster for the 2023 Starbucks Barista Championship, created in Adobe Illustrator to showcase illustration and layout design skills. Featuring custom vector art of coffee mugs, beans, and logos, the design uses freeform gradients, bold typography, and shape tools to highlight the event's prestige and excitement for both employees and guests.
