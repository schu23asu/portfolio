About the Designer

Hi I'm Jackson!
I'm a web designer based in Seattle, WA - drawn to structure, flow, and the little details that make a site feel just right.

I work with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript - often paired with tools like VS Code, Git, and a bit of pen-and-paper sketching to map things out. I enjoy crafting sites that balance function and personality, with a focus on clarity and usability.

From playful personal projects to more polished client work, I aim to build web experiences that are thoughtful, expressive, and easy to navigate.
